Private Comfort or Smart Investment

For Your Family Home

Enjoy hotel-style every morning. No more queuing for the bathroom. A private en-suite bathroom gives you and your family privacy, convenience and a real sense of home.

  • ★ Complete privacy for the main bedroom
  • ★ Increases daily comfort & property appeal
  • ★ Adds 5–8% to your home’s value in London
Families across Chelsea, Kensington and Notting Hill tell us this single change transformed their mornings. No more rushed bathroom schedules, no more arguments over hot water, and a noticeable increase in property value when you eventually sell. Recent Savills and Knight Frank reports (2025-2026) confirm en-suite bathroom additions in family homes deliver the highest satisfaction and resale uplift of any home improvement under £15k.
